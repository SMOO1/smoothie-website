sweet smoothies

Key Ingredients:

  • Banana

    great source of dietary fiber, potassium, and vitamin C. The addition of bananas is completely optional, I just like to add them because they make the smoothie creamy.

  • Organic Cow Milk

    good source of calcium, protein, and essential vitamins like vitamin D and vitamin B12.

  • Vanilla Yogurt

    provides protein, calcium, and probiotics, which help maintain your gut health.

Strawberry-Banana

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • two handfuls of strawberries
  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 1/2 litre cow milk 3.8% M.F.

Strawberries: high in essential nutrients like vitamin C, manganese, and antioxidants.

Tropical Bliss

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup vanilla yogurt
  • 3 chunks of mango
  • 3 chunks of pineapple
  • 3 chunks of passion fruit
  • 1/2 litre cow milk 3.8% M.F.

Mango: high in vitamins A and C, and a good source of dietary fiber.

Pineapple: provides vitamins A and manganese. Also has bromelain (mixture of enzymes), which helps with the process of digestion.

Passion Fruit: contains vitamins A and C, along with antioxidants (such as riboflavin and carotene) which help improve your bloodflow and retain the moisture in your skin.

Forest Berry

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup mixed-berry yogurt
  • 1 handful of raspberries
  • 1 handful of blueberries
  • 1 handful of blackberries
  • 1/2 litre cow milk 3.8% M.F.

Raspberry: high in vitamin C (supports your immune health) and fibers (which help with digestion).

Blueberry: great source of anthocyanins (hence the blue color), which support cognitive functions like your memory. Additionally, the berry's high source of vitamins and fiber help reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.

Blackberry: holds many antioxidants, like anthocyanins, which hold anti-inflammatory and anti-microbial properties that can help combat diabetes and different types of cancer.
